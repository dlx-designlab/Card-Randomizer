Usually you are trying to think about ideas for people right? So sometimes it is good to start with a person and think of an idea for them.

Take a card, on each card there is an image of a person and a name that describes their character. None of them are normal people but rather one of a range of unusual characters.

{% elif cards_folder == "context_cards" %}

Ideas are almost always located in a place, correct?

In this set of cards there are a wide range of different places and spaces, take a card and think what kind of ideas are inspired from that place. Maybe you can think of ideas specifically for that place, or the place on the card catalyzes a new idea.

There are 48 cards most are everyday spaces, but one set of 6 cards are of extreme environments.

{% elif cards_folder == "parameter_cards" %}

These cards are useful when you know what it is you are thinking about (e.g. a new type of kettle, or a new type of banking service).

Each of these cards has a different parameter on it, like price, weight, size, etc. Take a card and see if you can think of an idea that responds to that card.

e.g. you pick a super low costs card and you are thinking of a new type of kettle - can you think of an idea for a $1 kettle?
